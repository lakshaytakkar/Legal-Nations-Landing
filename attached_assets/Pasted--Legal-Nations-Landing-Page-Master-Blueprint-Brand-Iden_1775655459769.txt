# Legal Nations — Landing Page Master Blueprint

## Brand Identity & Design System

### Brand Positioning
**Tagline:** "One Platform. Every Country. Zero Compliance Stress."
**Sub-tagline:** "Register, File, and Stay Compliant — Anywhere in the World."

**Brand Promise:** Legal Nations is the world's most affordable all-in-one platform for global company registration, tax filing, and ongoing compliance. From Delaware to Dubai, Wyoming to Singapore — one dashboard handles everything so founders can focus on building.

**Voice:** Authoritative but accessible. No legal jargon. Short punchy sentences. Data-backed claims. Trust through transparency.

### Color System (Light Theme — Professional Blue)

```
--primary:        #1E40AF    (Deep Blue — buttons, headings, nav active)
--primary-light:  #3B82F6    (Medium Blue — links, hover states)  
--primary-subtle: #EFF6FF    (Ice Blue — section backgrounds, cards)
--accent:         #F59E0B    (Amber Gold — CTAs, badges, pricing highlights)
--accent-hover:   #D97706    (Dark Amber — CTA hover)
--dark:           #0F172A    (Slate 900 — body text)
--muted:          #64748B    (Slate 500 — secondary text)
--border:         #E2E8F0    (Slate 200 — dividers, card borders)
--surface:        #F8FAFC    (Slate 50 — alt section bg)
--white:          #FFFFFF    (Cards, hero bg)
--success:        #10B981    (Green — checkmarks, trust indicators)
--danger:         #EF4444    (Red — comparison "competitors" column)
```

### Typography
```
Headings:    "Plus Jakarta Sans" (Google Fonts — geometric, modern, premium feel)
Body:        "DM Sans" (Google Fonts — clean, highly readable)
```

### SECTION 12: FAQ Accordion
**Layout:** White bg, centered, max-width 800px

```
Heading: "Got Questions? We've Got Answers."
```

**FAQs:**

1. **How long does it take to register a company?**
   Most US formations are completed within 24-48 hours. International formations (Singapore, Dubai, UK) take 3-7 business days depending on jurisdiction.

2. **Do I need to be a US citizen to form a US LLC?**
   No. Anyone from any country can form a US LLC or C-Corp. You don't need a US address, SSN, or visa. We handle everything remotely.

3. **What's the difference between Delaware and Wyoming?**
   Delaware is preferred for VC-funded startups and complex corporate structures due to the Court of Chancery. Wyoming offers lower costs, stronger privacy, and $0 state income tax — ideal for solopreneurs and e-commerce.

4. **Are there any hidden fees?**
   Zero. Our pricing page shows exactly what you pay. State filing fees are passed through at cost with no markup.

5. **Do you help with bank account opening?**
   Yes. We assist with applications to Mercury, Wise, Relay, and other founder-friendly banks. Professional and Enterprise plans include priority banking support.

6. **Can I form companies in multiple countries?**
   Absolutely. Our Enterprise plan supports up to 3 countries with a unified compliance dashboard. Need more? Contact our team for custom plans.

7. **What ongoing compliance do I need after formation?**
   Depends on the state/country. We track everything for you — annual reports, franchise tax, registered agent renewals — and send reminders or auto-file on your behalf.

8. **Is Legal Nations cheaper than Firstbase or LegalZoom?**
   Yes. Our Wyoming LLC starts at $149 (Firstbase charges $399). We also offer multi-country support they don't. See our comparison table above.

## Key Copywriting Principles Used

1. **Headline Formula:** Benefit + Scope + Relief from pain
2. **No paragraphs over 2 lines** — everything is scannable
3. **Every section has a CTA** — no dead ends
4. **Social proof embedded everywhere** — numbers, ratings, testimonials, logos
5. **Competitor anchoring** — show our price next to theirs, always
6. **Country flags everywhere** — visual trust + global credibility
7. **Checklist format** — people love checking off what's included
8. **Urgency via announcement bar** — limited offer framing
9. **"From $XX" pricing** — low anchor point draws people in
10. **Office addresses** — physical presence = trust, especially for legal services
