2. The Collaboration — What We're Building Together
The Core Idea
USDrop AI users need a US LLC to:

Sell on Amazon, Walmart, eBay, and TikTok Shop (all require a US business entity)
Open a US bank account (Mercury, Relay, Chase) for receiving payouts
Access payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal Business, Shopify Payments)
Get personal asset protection (LLC separates personal and business liability)
Build US business credibility (US address, EIN, operating agreement)
Benefit from Wyoming/Delaware tax advantages
How It Works
USDrop has a dedicated LLC page inside the platform at /framework/my-llc (accessible to all logged-in users).
This page educates users on why they need a US LLC and presents two LegalNations packages at an exclusive 30% discount for USDrop members.
Users click through to LegalNations' website to complete the purchase and formation process.
USDrop's admin panel tracks LLC application status through a 7-stage pipeline (see below).
Automated SMS and email notifications are sent to users at each stage transition.

4. LLC Application Tracking Pipeline
Once a user purchases through LegalNations, USDrop tracks the application through a 7-stage pipeline in the admin panel. LegalNations should communicate stage transitions to USDrop so admins can update the status.

Stages (in order):
#	Stage	Description	Automated User Notification
1	Pending	Application received, payment confirmed	Welcome email + SMS
2	Filed	LLC documents filed with the state	"Your LLC has been filed" email + SMS
3	EIN Received	EIN (Tax ID) received from IRS	"Your EIN is ready" email + SMS
4	BOI Filed	Beneficial Ownership Information filed	"BOI filing complete" email + SMS
5	Bank Opened	US bank account successfully opened	"Bank account ready" email + SMS
6	Stripe Connected	Stripe payment gateway connected	"Payments are live" email + SMS
7	Complete	Everything done, fully operational	"Congratulations! All set" email + SMS
Key: USDrop sends automated emails and SMS to users at every stage change. LegalNations just needs to communicate the stage update to the USDrop admin team (via email, webhook, or shared dashboard — to be discussed).
